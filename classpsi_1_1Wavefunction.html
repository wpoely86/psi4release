void Wavefunction::semicanonicalize ( )
virtual

Semicanonicalizes ROHF orbitals, breaking the alpha-beta degeneracy On entrance, there's only one set of orbitals and orbital energies. On exit, the alpha and beta Fock matrices correspond to those in the semicanonical basis, and there are distinct alpha and beta C and epsilons, also in the semicanonical basis.

Reimplemented in psi::scf::ROHF.
