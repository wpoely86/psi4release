24 ** PSIFILES.H
25 **
26 ** This header file contains the definitions of the numbers assigned
27 ** to various binary files in PSI. This was created primarily to
28 ** help avoid conflicts in the numbering of new PSI files in developmental
29 ** programs but will grow to encompass some older binary files.
30 **
31 ** This additional level of abstraction will aid in the maintenance of
32 ** code. You are strongly encouraged to refer to files using these
33 ** definitions rather than the actual numbers; the numbers may change
34 ** in the future but the names will not.
35 **
36 ** Created by C. David Sherrill on 29 April 1998
37 **
38 ** Make sure that you comment any new additions to this, so they are inlined
39 ** into the manual as well as the psifiles.py python file. Note the format
40 ** of the existing comment markers, and keep comments on one line.
41 */
